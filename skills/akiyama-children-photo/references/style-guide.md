# Style Guide

Use this reference when a user wants closer art direction, batch consistency, or prompt repair for candid analog children's documentary images.

## Source Context

The user reference points to Ryoji Akiyama's China child-focused documentary work, often discussed through "Dear Old Days" and "Hello, Little Friends". Public descriptions emphasize early-1980s China, children in ordinary daily life, color documentary photographs, school breaks, games, beach play, lunch scenes, streets, and a rare sense of relaxed closeness.

Useful source pages:

- https://www.shashasha.co/en/book/china-1981-1982
- https://en.chinaculture.org/a/202307/13/WS64af5d0ba31035260b8162da.html

Do not copy a specific reference image or ask an image model to imitate a living photographer's exact style. Convert the reference into general visual grammar.

## Visual Grammar

- Viewpoint: low or child-height camera, close but not intrusive.
- Framing: 1:1, 4:3, or 3:2; allow cropped limbs, half-entered passersby, chairs, laundry, trees, signs, bicycles, and street clutter.
- Moment: mid-action and slightly imperfect; the child may glance at camera, ignore it, hide, laugh, eat, run, or pause between games.
- Light: natural daylight, shaded streets, soft tree shadows, window spill, no strong studio flash.
- Color: muted analog color, gray-green streets, warm skin under soft daylight, restrained reds and yellows, light underexposure.
- Texture: real skin texture, fine grain, dust or atmospheric haze, no glossy AI smoothness.
- Social context: public everyday scenes, adults or other children in the edges, real life continuing around the subject.

## Prompt Blocks

Start with the scene:

```text
1980s China, ordinary residential street, summer tree shade, children in everyday clothes
```

Add subject and action:

```text
a group of children chasing each other, spontaneous expressions, one child glancing briefly toward the camera
```

Add camera grammar:

```text
child-height documentary viewpoint, close distance, 35mm lens, natural light, slight motion blur, imperfect framing
```

Add environment:

```text
old bicycles, hand-painted signs, laundry, stools, passersby partly entering the frame, dusty pavement
```

Add film realism:

```text
muted analog color film, fine grain, mild underexposure, realistic skin texture, photojournalistic realism
```

## Negative Direction

Avoid:

- studio portrait, fashion editorial, glamour, commercial children campaign
- polished cinematic lighting, teal-orange grade, HDR, bokeh-heavy luxury look
- plastic skin, overly symmetrical faces, doll-like children
- empty perfect backgrounds or AI-clean streets
- sexualized, adultified, distressed, injured, or identifiable real children
- exact frame reconstruction from a known image

## Scenario Bank

- Schoolyard: children jump rope, line up for lunch, whisper during break, lean on single bars.
- Street: child on a small stool, elders seated nearby, bicycles and hand-painted signage.
- Riverside: children crouch at stone steps, catching fish, wet sandals, adults washing clothes in background.
- Rural courtyard: feeding chicks, old wooden doors, fruit trees, water basin, family clutter.
- Park or beach: running, kite string, sand or grass, distant family silhouettes.
- Canteen: enamel bowls, lunch boxes, benches, noisy but warm midday light.
